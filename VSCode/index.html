Chicken Curry

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Onion
  • Peppers
  • Coconut milk
  • Chicken stock
  • Curry powder
  • Rice

Procedure

  1. Add 1.5 cups of rice to rice maker, fill water and cook.
  2. Cut chciken and fry over medium heat. Once cooked move to side.
  3. Dice onion and fry until golden. Add diced peppers and fry for 2mins.
  4. Add 2 tbls spoons of curry power and fry.
  5. Add 1 cup of chicken stock.
  6. Bring to boil, then allow simmer.
  7. Serve.

Chicken Fajitas

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Onion
  • Peppers
  • Sour cream
  • Cheese
  • Salsa
  • Tortilla wraps

Procedure

  1. Cut chicken and fry over medium heat. Once cooked move to side.
  2. Dice onion and fry until golden. Add diced peppers and fry for 2mins.
  3. Heat tortilla wraps.
  4. Add chicken and veg to wraps.
  5. Top wrap with salsa, sour cream and cheese.
  6. Enjoy.

Spagetti Bolognese

Ingredients

  • Mince beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Tomato paste
  • Spagetti

Procedure

  1. Fry onion and minced garlic over a medium heat.
  2. Add beef to frying pan and cook until brown.
  3. Add tomato sauce and paste to pan. Simmer for 10mins.
  4. Cook spagetti over high heat.
  5. Serve beef on a bed of spagetti.
